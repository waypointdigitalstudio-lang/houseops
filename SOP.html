ControlDeck

Standard Operating Procedure — Inventory & Asset Management
For: Operations Staff Sites: Bally's Tiverton & Bally's Lincoln Platform: iOS & Android

Getting Started

When you open the app you will be prompted to sign in. Use the email and password provided by your administrator. Once signed in, the app is scoped to your site — you will only see inventory, alerts, and disposals for your location.

The app has five tabs along the bottom:

  • Inventory — your stock list
  • Alerts — low and out-of-stock notifications
  • Scan — barcode scanner
  • Disposal — asset disposal log
  • Settings — your account and preferences

Inventory Tab

Viewing Inventory

The inventory list shows all items at your site. Each card displays the item name, location, and current stock level. Items at or below their minimum quantity will show a LOW or OUT badge in red.

Use the search bar to find items by name. Tap Low Stock Only to filter to items that need attention. Use the sort buttons to order by name or stock level.

Adding a New Item

Tap the + button in the top right. Fill in:

  • Item Name (required)
  • Current Quantity (required)
  • Minimum Quantity — the threshold that triggers a low stock alert
  • Location — where the item is physically stored
  • Barcode — optional, used with the scanner
  • Notes — any additional information

Tap Add Item to save.

Editing an Item

Tap anywhere on an item card to open its detail screen. Tap Edit Details to make changes to the name, quantities, location, or barcode. Tap Save when done or Cancel to discard.

Adjusting Stock

From the item detail screen, tap Adjust Stock to add or remove quantity. Select Add or Remove, enter the amount, and optionally add a note explaining the reason. Every adjustment is logged in the item's movement history.

Disposing an Item (Partial or Full)

Tap the orange archive icon on any inventory item to log a disposal. Fill in:

  • Item Name — pre-filled, edit if needed
  • Amount — how many units are being disposed (current stock is shown above this field)
  • Reason — Broken, Damaged, Obsolete, Lost, or Other
  • Who is disposing (required)
  • Model, Vendor, Value, Age, Notes — fill in as applicable

Tap Confirm Disposal. The disposed quantity will be subtracted from the item's current stock. The item stays in inventory — it is not deleted.

Deleting an Item

Tap the red trash icon on an item. A 5-second undo banner will appear at the bottom of the screen. Tap Undo to cancel. If you do nothing, the item is permanently deleted after 5 seconds.

Importing from CSV

Tap Import CSV to bulk-load inventory from a spreadsheet. The file must be a .csv with columns for name, quantity, minimum quantity, and location. Contact your administrator for the correct template.

Toners Tab

Within the Inventory tab, tap Toners to switch to the toner sub-tab.

Toners

Each toner card shows the model, color, current quantity, and minimum quantity. Tap the edit icon to update details. Tap the trash icon to delete (with 5-second undo).

Tap Add Toner to create a new record. Fill in:

  • Model Name (required)
  • Color — Black, Cyan, Magenta, Yellow, or Other
  • Quantity (required)
  • Minimum Quantity
  • Printer — which printer this toner is for

Printers

Tap Printers under the Toners tab to see all printers at your site. Printers can be imported via CSV or added manually. Each printer can be linked to a toner so stock levels are tracked together.

Alerts Tab

Stock Alerts

The Alerts tab shows all items that are currently low or out of stock, sorted by severity (OUT first, then LOW). The tab icon shows a live badge count of how many items need attention.

Each alert card shows the item name, location, current quantity vs. minimum, and a status badge. Tap an alert to dismiss it. A dismissed alert will reappear if the quantity changes again.

Tap Generate Reorder List to export a CSV of all current alerts sorted by severity — useful for placing orders.

Activity Log

Tap Activity at the top of the Alerts tab to see a full log of every change made across your site's inventory. Filter by time period (Today / 7 Days / 30 Days / All) and by action type (Added, Edited, Disposed, etc.). Tap Export CSV to download the log.

Scan Tab

Point the camera at any barcode. The app will look it up in your inventory.

  • If found — you are taken directly to that item's detail screen where you can adjust stock or view history.
  • If not found — you are offered the option to add it as a new inventory item or as a new toner.

Disposal Tab

The Disposal tab is a standalone log of all disposed assets at your site, separate from inventory adjustments.

Viewing Records

Each record shows the item name, reason (color-coded badge), quantity, who disposed it, and the date.

Adding a Record Manually

Tap Add Record if you need to log a disposal that wasn't done through the inventory screen. Fill in all applicable fields. Item Name and Disposed By are required.

Exporting Records

Tap Export CSV to download all disposal records. After the export completes, you will be asked if you want to delete all records from the app. Tap Delete All to clear or Keep Records to leave them in place.

Deleting All Records

Tap Delete All at any time to permanently clear all disposal records for your site. You will be asked to confirm before anything is deleted.

Settings Tab

Theme

Choose Light, Dark, or System to match your device preference.

Your Site & Role

Displays your current site assignment and role (staff or admin). If you see "No site assigned," contact your administrator.

Device Registration

Register your device to receive push notifications for low-stock and out-of-stock alerts. Tap Register, enter a label for your device (e.g. "John's iPhone"), and save. You can reset your token here if notifications stop working.

Alert Preferences

Toggle which alerts you receive — Low Stock, Out of Stock, and Restocked. You can also toggle sound and vibration.

Export Inventory

Tap Export Inventory to CSV to download a full snapshot of your site's current inventory.

Logging Out

Scroll to the bottom and tap Log Out. You will be asked to confirm.

Admin Panel (Admin Role Only)

The Admin tab is only visible to users with the admin role.

Managing Users

The user list shows everyone across both sites. Tap any user to:

  • Change their role — promote to admin or demote to staff
  • Send a password reset email
  • Delete their profile — removes the user from the app

Reassigning a User's Site

Select a user and select the new site. Check Also update this user's device tokens to move their push notifications to the new site at the same time. Tap Save Site Assignment.

Tips & Reminders

Low stock badge on the Alerts tab updates in real time — if someone else on your team adjusts stock, the count updates automatically.

Undo is available for 5 seconds after any delete. If you miss it, the item is gone permanently.

Disposal does not delete — it subtracts the disposed quantity from stock and logs the record. The item stays in your inventory at the reduced count.

All data is site-scoped — staff at Tiverton cannot see Lincoln's inventory and vice versa.

If something looks wrong, check the Activity Log on the Alerts tab — every change is tracked with who made it and when.
