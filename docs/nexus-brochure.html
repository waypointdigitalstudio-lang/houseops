Platform overview

Nexus is a real-time mobile operations platform built for multi-site teams. From inventory levels and toner stock to preventative maintenance schedules and radio part tracking, Nexus brings every operational workflow into a single, role-secured app — with live push notifications, CSV reporting, and cloud-synced data across every device on your network.

Core modules
📦
Inventory
Real-time stock tracking with low-stock & out-of-stock alerts and CSV export.
🔧
Preventative Maintenance
Quarterly PM checklists with overdue tracking, badges, and reset workflow.
🖨️
Printers & Toners
Full printer fleet registry with toner level management and model tracking.
📻
Radio Parts
Two-way radio parts inventory with per-site stock levels and alerts.
🔔
Alerts & Analytics
Push notifications, activity log, date-range filters, and visual analytics charts.
🗑️
Asset Disposal
Auditable records for retired hardware — track what was removed and when.
📖
Vendor Directory
Contacts, vendors, and Lincoln Tech partners organized by site.
🔐
Role-Based Access
Admin and staff tiers — each site sees only its own data, enforced server-side.
